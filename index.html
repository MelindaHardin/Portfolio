About

Melinda was born and raised in Louisville, Kentucky where she and her four siblings (one being her twin sister) developed and appreciated the hospitable customs of the South. After graduating from Jeffersontown High School, Melinda relocated to Bowling Green, Kentucky to attend the University of the Hilltoppers, Western Kentucky University. While at Western, she studied journalism. She then transferred to Sullivan University in her home city of Louisville, where she obtained an Associate’s of Science degree in Paralegal Studies in 2006. Melinda later returned to Western Kentucky University where she completed and obtained a Bachelor’s of Science in Organizational Leadership.

Although Melinda values the traditions of the South and Midwest, she also appreciates the diversity that comes with living in a larger city. While living in Miami, Florida, Melinda was a promotional model, paralegal, and owned and operated a home staging business, Existing Compliments. These opportunities encouraged Melinda to peruse other passions outside of modeling and a paralegal career. As Existing Compliments expanded its services and materials, Melinda took it upon herself to update the company website to reflect the changes. Successfully teaching herself the very basics HTML and CCS.

In 2014, Melinda married her Louisville native boyfriend of 8 years, Joey Hardin, in the Smokey Mountains of Gatlinburg Tennessee. Melinda and Joey have since relocated to Dallas, Texas and then to the Kansas City Metro area, where they have lived since April of 2016. Melinda and Joey enjoy traveling, socializing and hiking in the beautiful Kansas City area parks. Melinda still practices her interrior education passions with the Airbnb they currently opperate in their home. She is also learning to put her education passions to use as a website developer.

Crystal Collector

Use of jQuery which dynamically updates the HTML. Four crystals are displayed as buttons on the page. The player will be shown a random number at the start of them game. When the player clicks on a crystal, it will add a specific amount of points to the player's total score. The player wins if the score matches the random number and loses if their score goes above the number.



Trivia Game

Use of JavaScript timers and jQuery that manipulates the HTML. The player will have a limited amount of time to answer eight questions. One question is displayed at a time and moves on to the next question when the time runs out. At the end of the game, the player is shown how many questions were correct, incorrect, and unanswered.

Giphy

Dynamically populate the HTML page with buttons and gifs of your choice. Use of Bootstrap, API (Giphy), JavaScript and jQuery. The user enters a topic in the search bar which creates a button. They can then click on a still image which will play the gif. The gif will stop if the image is clicked a second time.


Train Scheduler

Use of Google Firebase, Moments.js and JavaScript. The user submits the name of a train and a destination. Moments.js retrieves and manipulates the information to display the arrival of the next train and how many minutes remain intil they will arrive at the station.

Lost & Found

Use of MySQL, Sequelize and Handlebars. A group project. The user will add a lost item to the table. The item is added to the "lost" MySQL table. If found, the user will select the button which then updates the item and places it on the "found" MySQL table.

Click Game

Use of REACT. Renders different images which listen for click events. The user's score is tracked and reset to 0 if the same image is clicked more than once. With each click, the page shuffles the images.
