About Me

Hi there! My name is Melinda! I was born and raised in Louisville, Kentucky. Louisville is where I developed and have come to appreciate hospitable customs of the South. After graduating from Jeffersontown High School , I relocated to Bowling Green, Kentucky to attend , Western Kentucky University. While at Western, I studied journalism, later changing my interest to the legal field. With a new passion, I transferred to Sullivan University back in Louisville, where I obtained an Associate’s of Science degree in Paralegal Studies in 2006. I later returned to Western Kentucky University and obbtained a Bachelor’s of Science in Organizational Leadership.

Although I value the traditions of the South and Midwest, I also appreciates the diversity that comes with living in a larger city. While living in Miami, Florida, I was a promotional model, paralegal, and owned and operated a home staging business, Existing Compliments. These opportunities encouraged Me to peruse other passions outside of modeling and a legal career. As Existing Compliments expanded it's services and materials, I took it upon herself to update the company's website to reflect the changes, teaching myself the very basics HTML, CSS and WordPress

In 2014, I married fellow Louisville native Joey Hardin in the Smokey Mountains of Gatlinburg Tennessee. My husband and I have since relocated to Dallas, Texas and settling in the Kansas City Metro area, where we have lived since April of 2016. I enjoy traveling, socializing and hiking in the beautiful Kansas City area parks, and gardening. I still practices my interrior education passions with the Airbnb we currently opperate in our home, which I am proud to say is the highest rated Airbnb in Johnson County!
